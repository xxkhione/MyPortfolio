War Card Game App

During my first quarter at Neumont College of Computer Science, I took Intro to Computer Science. This class expanded a little further than the knowledge I went in with. We worked with Android Studio and Java to create event driven applications. During the fourth week, the class was assigned a "create you own app" lab. I decided to recreate War the card game. I used imageViews to create the cards as well as buttons and game logic to tie it all together.
